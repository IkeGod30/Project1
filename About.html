About Us

History

Zebra Consulting was conceptualized in response to a widespread need to provide businesses with desperately needed technologies for increase efficiency and innovative approach towards seamless business processess.

It all started with a visit to a friend's struggling business which was at the verge of bankruptcy in New York City during the 2008 financial crisis. The conversation about how the said business ended up in distress and couldn't weather the crisis in the economy birthed a journey to help businesses avert the situations that put the business in jeopardy

Milestones

Zebra Consulting solutions have helped a total of 216 businesses in 32 states and Canada fortify themselves against situations that would otherwise cause serious adverse outcomes. Zebra has successfully guided 52 businesses away from imminent backruptcy. It has also managed to transform 7 businesses that had gone through bankruptcy into post-liquidation acquisition which resulted in new successful ventures.
